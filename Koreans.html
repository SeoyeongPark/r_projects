Create graph

The graph shows that people earn around 1,000,000 KRW in age 20’s and it keeps increasing. The income peaks in 50’s, about 3,000,000 KRW. However, after that, income keeps decreasing, finally, 70’s people earn less amount than they earned in 20’s.
